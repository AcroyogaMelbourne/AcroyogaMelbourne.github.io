Acroyoga is acrobatics with a yogic consciousness, no experience is necessary to come and play. Jams are informal gatherings to play and practice. Classes develop your Acroyoga practice in a more formal settings. And Workshops are great for deepen your practice. Join our Facebook group, follow us on Twitter or start sharing #AcroyogaOttawa on Instagram or pinning inspiration to Pintrest. Fork us on GitHub to setup a similar Calendar for your own Acroyoga Community.
